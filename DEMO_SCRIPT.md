# CivicLens Demo Script

**Duration:** 2–3 minutes

## 1. Introduction (Landing Page)
* **Visual:** Start on the CivicLens Landing Page.
* **Talking Point:** "Welcome to CivicLens. Every day, citizens spot civic issues like potholes and broken streetlights, but cities struggle to prioritize them. CivicLens transforms raw citizen reports into verified, structured case files for fast civic action."

## 2. Citizen Experience (Reporting)
* **Visual:** Log in as a citizen. Click "Report Issue".
* **Talking Point:** "Let's say I'm walking in my neighborhood and spot a dangerous pothole. I simply open CivicLens, upload a photo, and the app captures the location."
* **Action:** Upload a sample pothole image, add a brief description, and submit.
* **Talking Point:** "Behind the scenes, our Civic Intelligence engine analyzes the evidence, assesses the safety risk, and prioritizes the case."

## 3. Civic Transparency (Public Board & Map)
* **Visual:** Navigate to the Issue Detail page of the newly created report.
* **Talking Point:** "Here is the generated case file. You can see the photo proof, the AI-generated Civic Intelligence Summary, and the assigned priority score."
* **Visual:** Navigate to the Civic Map.
* **Talking Point:** "Citizens can explore all reported issues across their community on this responsive civic map, ensuring full transparency."

## 4. Admin Experience (Operations Command Center)
* **Visual:** Log out and log back in as an Admin (or open another browser window). Navigate to the Admin Dashboard.
* **Talking Point:** "Now, let's switch to the perspective of a city official. This is the Civic Operations Command Center."
* **Visual:** Open the Case Queue and click on the newly reported pothole.
* **Talking Point:** "Officials have a clear, prioritized queue. Inside the case file, the admin sees the AI analysis and can take immediate action."

## 5. Escalation & Resolution
* **Visual:** Click "Prepare Escalation Brief".
* **Talking Point:** "For high-priority issues, the admin can instantly generate a formal Escalation Brief to alert senior officials."
* **Visual:** Scroll down to "Submit Resolution Proof". Upload an 'after' photo and mark it as resolved.
* **Talking Point:** "Once a crew fixes the issue, they submit resolution proof—an 'after' photo. CivicLens verifies this resolution."

## 6. Conclusion (Resolved Case)
* **Visual:** Show the public Issue Detail page with the Before/After resolution view.
* **Talking Point:** "The public case file is updated with visual proof of the fix, closing the loop with the community. CivicLens: From local proof to verified civic action."
